Collective Manifest

We operate under the philosophy that digital systems and physical goods should exist in dialogue. Our ventures represent nodes in a decentralized network of design, production, and distribution. We do not mass-produce; we build limited runs, run live experiments, and document our source code.

Core Principles

  • 01 /
    Tactile Overload

    Whether ink on cotton or pixels on a screen, texture, noise, and friction create depth.

  • 02 /
    Open Architecture

    Our codebases and processes are shared openly, inviting adaptation and analysis.

  • 03 /
    Finite Output

    Scarcity drives appreciation. When a batch or auction is complete, it does not repeat.
